Description

Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1% is a highly concentrated serum with vitamins and minerals that counteract acne and other impurities. A high concentration of 10% of niacinamide (vitamin B3) in combination with zinc salt from pyrrolidone carboxylic acid balances sebum production. Niacinamide is a multifunctional super-ingredient that reduces and treats black pores, uneven skin tones, fine lines and a weakened skin barrier. Niacinamide also has an anti-inflammatory effect. Zinc helps to reduce the skin's sebum production, so that it feels less oily. The serum is vegan and has not been tested on.

Note:
If you use products with vitamin C as part of everyday skin care, these products should not be combined. Ideally, vitamin C should be used in the evening and niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1% during the day. Otherwise, niacinamide can affect the effect of vitamins C.

Note:
Although niacinamide and zinc reduce the visibility of acne and balance sebum quality, it alone is not considered a treatment for acne. In persistent acne-related conditions, benzoyl peroxide and / or retinoic acid are recommended. Niacinamide + Zinc can be used together with other acne treatments to achieve the best possible results.
