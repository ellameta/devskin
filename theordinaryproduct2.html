Description

Glycolic Acid 7% Toning Solution is an exfoliating face toner. This 7% glycolic acid toner gives a gentle exfoliation and leaves a clear skin full of irresistible glow. The structure of the skin will achieve an improved effect with regular use over time. The toner contains a derivative of Tasmanian pepper and helps to minimize irritations associated with the use of acid. Be aware that this derivative is of plant origin and therefore varies according to the color of the season, which may prove to have a visible effect on the color of the toner from season to season. The formula is fortified with ginseng root and aloe vera for visible glow and soothing effect on the skin. The product is vegan and has not been tested on animals.

Note:
The product contains alpha hydroxy acid which can increase your skin's sensitivity to the sun and especially the risk of sunburn. Use sunscreen daily when using this product.

Note:
Although niacinamide and zinc reduce the visibility of acne and balance sebum quality, it alone is not considered a treatment for acne. In persistent acne-related conditions, benzoyl peroxide and / or retinoic acid are recommended. Niacinamide + Zinc can be used together with other acne treatments to achieve the best possible results.
